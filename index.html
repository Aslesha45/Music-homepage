About Us

Dreat Music Company is a rising creative powerhouse based in Bangladesh. We focus on nurturing young talents and producing impactful music that resonates across South Asia, with a special focus on collaborations between Bangladesh and India.

Our Artists

Dana - Solo Artist

Dana is a talented Bangladeshi artist who gained national recognition through music festivals and competitions. Her expressive voice and original compositions make her a standout talent in the region.

SHIKHOR - Boy Group

SHIKHOR blends hip-hop and rock with storytelling and choreography. This five-member group includes four Bangladeshi and one Indian member, showcasing the creative power of regional collaboration.

Bashi - Instrumental Artist

Bashi fuses traditional Bangladeshi and Indian sounds with modern beats. Their album "Beyond Time" has been praised for bringing regional instrumental innovation to the global stage.
